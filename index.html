About US

EnactusCivil work Corporation that has VISION to create better, more sustainable Egyptian communities. MISSION engage the next generation of entrepreneurial leaders to use innovation and business principles for the improvement of the Egyptian community. “Enactus brings together the top leaders of today and tomorrow from around the world sharing the purpose of enabling progress through entrepreneurial action”

Who we are?

Enactus is an international non-profit organization that uses the power of entrepreneurial action to improve the quality of life and standard of living for people in need.

What we do?

Enactus provides a platform for university students to collaborate with business and academic leaders in development of entrepreneurial based projects that empower people to have better life.

Why we do?

To enable progress through entrepreneurial action to transform lives and shape a better more sustainable world.

Sponsers

Enactus sponsors play an essential role in advancing a team’s work to improve lives, strengthen communities and develop socially responsible business leaders, while realizing the business advantages that come from being a part of the Enactus network. It’s a partnership that provides a valuable addition to a company’s CSR portfolio, as well as the opportunity for employee involvement, student recruitment, executive networking and brand awareness. These opportunities are structured around annual giving levels and individual sponsorships of specific program initiatives and events.
